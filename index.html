Queens College(CUNY) | New York

Computer science, 2019

As a transfer student, I entered Queens college for the first time in 2014. My previous major was accounting, and my friend told me that accounting is a popular major, which is easy and simple. But after a year of study, I found that I am not good at dealing with numbers, economic and financial matters' things. I did not get good results in almost all subjects. This year of study and life made me feel that I was not suitable to continue studying, so I chose to suspend school. When I was 28, I suddenly realized that I can only get a better life through study. After serious thinking, I returned to Queens college and changed my major to computer science. I chose this new major because I have a great interest in computer science.
